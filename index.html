Opening Conversation
Yuki Tanaka
On silence as a creative act

Yuki Tanaka has spent two decades refusing to separate what she paints from what she composes. In this opening conversation — recorded in her studio in Tokyo over three evenings — she speaks about the moment she realised that a blank canvas and a rest in a score are the same gesture. We also talk about failure, translation, and why she has never shown her earliest work to anyone.

Location Tokyo, Japan
